You are a particular elite team member and are called into battle. Your weapons are ready, and your equipment is prepared. All your team needs is you. Guerrillas io is a multiplayer battling game that you can play online. Game maps are inspired by Counter-Strike, the best-known video game, and may look familiar to you! Time is running out. If you are ready, let's start!

How to Play Guerrillas io?

  • Guerrillas io offers you an online battling environment. You participate in a game by joining one of the available rooms or by creating a new room in the online option.
  • On the left side of the menu screen, you will see a box called "Rooms." You can see available rooms or open a new one when you click here.
  • By creating a new room, you can choose how many players can join the battle, the game mode, the road time, the goal, the bots and the map. All is up to your vision!
  • Also, from the menu, you can see and adjust your equipment by entering the "Armoury" section, obtain power-ups from the "Perks" section, or decide what to give as a death message from the "Kill Message."

Game Modes

Before we start, we need to choose a game mode. Game modes are as follows:

  • Team Deathmatch: The players are divided into 2 teams in which they are battling against each other.
  • Free for All: In this game mode, all players are alone. Create your own strategy to survive.
  • Capture the Flag: This is, again, a teamwork gameplay. You must take the flag from the enemy's base and return it to your location.
  • Gun Game: This game mode is a mode that allows new weapons to be unlocked. You start with the simplest weapon and continue by updating the weapons.
  • Arcade Doom: You are invincible in Arcade Doom, as you can possess guns and grenade launchers.

Controls

  • Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys to move
  • Use the Left mouse click to fire
  • Use the Right mouse click to aim
  • Use the Space key to jump
  • Use the Q key to slide while running
  • Use the Left Shift to run
  • Use 1, 2, 3 to change weapon
  • Use the E or F key to interact/pick up a weapon
  • Use the G key to throw available grenades
  • Use the H key to use an available medkit
  • Use the T key to chat
  • Use the L key to toggle the cursor lock
  • Use the C key to crouch
  • Use the Hold Tab to show the scoreboard
  • Use the ESC to pause
