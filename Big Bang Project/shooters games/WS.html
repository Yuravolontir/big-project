In the ocean of shoot-'em-all games, finding an online shooter that meets your console gamer expectations can feel impossible. After playing legends like Counter-Strike or Far Cry, other online FPS games may seem bland, lacking an immersive story. But believe it or not, WarStrike will give you the taste of playing Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, all from the comfort of your browser.

WarStrike's campaign mode takes you deep into a critical mission as an elite MI-6 Special Soldier. You'll complete duties across 24 chapters, unraveling a story of global threats, deadly viruses, and seeking justice. Each chapter unfolds like a blockbuster action movie with its cleverly designed scenario and diverse missions.

Not ready for a long-term commitment to another shooter game? Try quick multiplayer battles; they'll give you some clues about how epic WarStrike is. So either way, you've got a game that delivers on all fronts. Have fun striking!

Controls

  • Move: WASD keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Left Shift: Run
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R key
  • CrouChange Weapon: 1 and 2 keysch: C key
  • Melee Attack: Q key or 3 key
  • PickThrow Grenade: G key or 4 Key-up Weapon: E
  • Use Medkit: H
  • Use Pill: J
  • Unlock Cursor: L
  • Pause Game: Tab key
