Legendary Sniper is an online sniper shooter game where you follow the commands of your general. War has begun! You are the most talented sniper of the squad. The fate of the battle is in your hands. Complete the missions. Prove your legendary aiming skills to your commander. Take your sniper rifle now. You need to eliminate the enemies with headshots!

How to Play Legendary Sniper

  • Hit the play button and select a level on the map.
  • We suggest you start with the first level to adjust to the gameplay.
  • While the first levels only require beginner-level skills, things get messier in the later levels.
  • Climb up the roof after level selection. Get your commands.
  • You need to take them off with headshots. Right-click to aim your target.
  • Hold your breath and left-click to shoot when you find the perfect window.
  • Repeat until you fulfill your tasks and get to the next one!
  • Make precise shots and honor your squad!

Need Some Tips?

  • When you enter the arena, have a brief look at your targets. Decide which ones are fuss-free and which are not.
  • Remember, the easy targets are those nearest you or who move less frequently.
  • The soldiers who run constantly and hide behind barricades or other objects require more focus and patience.
  • You don't have infinite ammo. So, take your shots wisely.
  • You are free to take your time. There is no limit on that. However, if you idle around too much, your health level will drop because of the attacks.
  • If you consume all your ammo before performing your duty, you need to start over.

Controls

  • Right Click: To aim
  • Left Click: To shoot
