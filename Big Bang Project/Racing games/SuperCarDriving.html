Super Car Driving is an online car game where you get to drive super-performing high-end models like Ferrari, Lamborghini, Porsche and more.

This online driving game offers a full package with 3 game modes that can appeal to every taste. Adrenaline seekers can enjoy completing stunt challenges while skill gamers maneuver their supercars in the lots of parking levels. Meanwhile, free spirits can hit the road on a GTA ViceCity-like map and test their sports cars' performance. All 3 in one game. So, what are you waiting for? Start playing it! Happy driving, gamers!

Controls

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Look Back: B

How to Play Super Car Driving

  • Choose a car among 13 supercars, pick a game mode and start driving.
  • Drive your car using WASD or arrow keys. Press space to pull handbrakes and initiaete drifts.
  • There are 4 epic camera modes you can pick among: first-person driver, cinematic rear wheel view, third person and top-down views. Press C to switch between views.
  • Complete tasks to rack up coins and buy more agile and dynamic-looking cars.
  • If you'd like, you can modify your car in the garage, add some texture to the coat, change the colors and lights, or even buy spoilers.

Stunt Levels

Unleash your inner daredevil with 20 exhilarating stunt challenges set in breathtakingly surreal landscapes. Each challenge is designed to test the limits of your vehicle, featuring mind-bending ramps, intricate loops, and gravity-defying twists. Get ready to drive through epic environments that challenge your precision and skill. This mode will push both you and your supercar to the edge.

Parking Challenges

Think you're a parking pro? Test your skills with agile, hard-to-control cars in challenge! Tackle 22 intense levels of precision parking tasks that will push your control and accuracy to the limit. All levels are unlocked from the beginning so you can choose the difficulty. Follow the red arrows to find the designated parking spot. The map changes in each level introducing new places and tricky obstacles. Airport, city street, mall parking lot and many more. Explore them all and collect in-game coins!

Free Roam

Drive in the city in free roam mode and discover the open roads with light traffic at your leisure. If you're interested, you can take on fun missions like racing through speed cameras. Or else you can be a taxi driver and collect passengers just like you did in GTA before. Each task comes with rewards determined by its level of difficulty.
